About Me

The Hacker Behind the Keyboard 🎭

Hey there! I'm a self-taught web designer and security researcher who fell in love with the web at 14 — not just building it, but understanding every hidden corner of it.

When I'm not hunting bugs or pushing pixels, you'll find me writing CTF writeups or teaching the next generation of ethical hackers.
